Moo-tion

Camera-Based Herd Management System

Moo-tion is a computer-vision-based system to support data-driven dairy farming. It detects estrus, predicts calving, and monitors cow activity using visual tags. Real-time alerts are sent when a cow is in heat, near calving, or missing—helping farmers save time and improve animal welfare.

  • Automatic estrus detection
  • Calving prediction
  • Activity tracking & missing alerts

Overview

CS491 is the capstone design course at Bilkent University where small teams (3–5 students) design, implement, and deliver an applied engineering project under faculty supervision. The course emphasizes practical system design, teamwork, documentation, and regular deliverables: project registration, innovation assessment, specification, analysis, and a final demo. Projects are expected to produce reproducible artifacts (reports, code, and demos) and follow good software and research practices.

For the official course description and policies, see the CS491 course page: CS491 — Course Page
